  • Early - > premature
  • Easy - > effortless
  • Everywhere - > ubiquitous
  • Expect - > anticipate
  • Expensive - > extravagant

  • Funny - > hilarious

  • Good - > magnificent
  • Happy - > elated/over the moon
  • Hard - > difficult/challenging/demanding/arduous/strenuous
